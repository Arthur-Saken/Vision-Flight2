ABOUT

At Vision-Flight, we embark on a creative journey where wood and metal converge, crafting exquisite tables and sculptures that elevate the essence of artistic expression. Guided by our unwavering vision, we blend masterful craftsmanship with boundless imagination, striving to create captivating pieces that resonate with our clients' deepest desires.

With meticulous attention to detail and an unwavering commitment to quality, we transform raw materials into tangible works of art that inspire awe and evoke a profound sense of wonder.

Footer Banner Section

Mikhail Bayder, a skilled artisan of wood and metal sculpting, draws inspiration from the captivating realms of Slavic and Norse mythology. With deft hands and a keen eye, he brings forth the essence of these ancient tales, crafting breathtaking designs that intertwine the raw beauty of nature with the mysticism of gods and mythical creatures. Mikhail's sculptures, depicting stoic warriors, ethereal goddesses, and adorned creatures, evoke a sense of wonder and invite viewers to immerse themselves in a world where time-honored legends come alive. Through his art, Mikhail creates gateways to a realm where myth and craftsmanship converge, reminding us of the enduring power and transformative nature of artistic expression.
